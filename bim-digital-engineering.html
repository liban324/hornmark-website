BIM & Digital Engineering

Overview

Pioneering Digital Engineering in Somalia

Horn Mark Engineering Services embraces Building Information Modelling (BIM) as a core component of modern engineering and construction project delivery. Through BIM-enabled workflows, we provide clients with improved project coordination, reduced construction risks, enhanced cost control, and optimized asset lifecycle management.

As one of the first engineering firms in Somalia to adopt BIM workflows, HMD is bringing the power of digital engineering to infrastructure projects across the Horn of Africa. Our BIM specialists work alongside the engineering, construction management, and project management teams to create intelligent 3D models that serve as the single source of truth throughout the project lifecycle.

The adoption of BIM allows clients to minimize design conflicts, improve project coordination, reduce construction risks, and make more informed decisions — delivering better outcomes from design through to facility management and long-term asset maintenance.

BIM Maturity

HMD delivers BIM Level 2 compliant workflows for federated model collaboration, and works towards Level 3 (integrated, cloud-hosted models) on suitable projects. Our BIM outputs comply with ISO 19650 and client-specific BIM Execution Plans (BEP).

BIM Dimensions

From 3D to nD — BIM Across Dimensions

3D BIM

3D Digital Models

Intelligent 3D models for architectural, structural, and MEP disciplines — the foundation of all BIM workflows and coordination.

4D BIM

Time & Construction Sequencing

Linking 3D model elements to project schedules to visualize construction sequences, optimize phasing, and identify programme risks.

5D BIM

Cost Planning & Estimation

Connecting model geometry to cost data for automated quantity extraction, cost planning, and budget tracking throughout design development.

6D BIM

Sustainability & Energy Analysis

Using BIM models for energy performance analysis, carbon assessment, and sustainability optimization of building and infrastructure designs.

7D BIM

Asset & Facilities Management

Handover of information-rich models to asset owners for facilities management, maintenance scheduling, and lifecycle cost analysis.

Clash Detection

Multi-Discipline Coordination

Federated model clash detection between architectural, structural, and MEP disciplines — eliminating design conflicts before construction begins.

Why BIM?

Key Benefits of BIM-Enabled Delivery

Fewer Design Conflicts

Clash detection identifies interdisciplinary clashes before construction, reducing costly on-site changes and rework.

Better Cost Control

5D BIM enables accurate quantity take-offs and real-time cost tracking as design evolves, improving budget certainty.

Improved Collaboration

A single federated model ensures all project stakeholders — designers, contractors, clients — work from the same information.

Faster Project Delivery

4D scheduling and visual sequencing optimise construction programmes and help teams anticipate and avoid delays.

Enhanced Client Understanding

3D visualizations and walkthroughs help clients understand and approve designs before investment is committed.

Better Asset Management

7D BIM provides facility managers with accurate, structured asset data for effective maintenance and lifecycle planning.
