Overview

Expert Engineering at Every Project Phase

Horn Mark Design Consultant Ltd. provides comprehensive engineering consultancy services covering all phases of project development. Our multidisciplinary team of engineers and technical specialists deliver precise, innovative, and cost-effective engineering solutions tailored to the specific needs of each project and client.

From initial concept and feasibility studies through detailed design, procurement support, and technical oversight, HMD ensures that every engineering deliverable meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and sustainability. Our engineering consultancy work spans roads and transport, water supply, buildings, energy systems, and urban infrastructure throughout Somalia and the Horn of Africa.

We work closely with government agencies, international development partners, NGOs, and private sector clients to deliver projects that are technically sound, financially viable, and aligned with international best practices.

Standards & Compliance

All HMD engineering deliverables are produced in compliance with applicable international standards including Eurocode, BS standards, ASTM, and donor-specific technical guidelines from the World Bank, AfDB, EU, and UN agencies.

Our Approach

Engineering Process

01

Project Inception & Needs Assessment

We begin with thorough engagement with the client to understand project objectives, constraints, stakeholder needs, and available baseline data. This phase sets the foundation for all subsequent technical work.

02

Feasibility & Options Analysis

We evaluate multiple technical solutions against technical, economic, environmental, and social criteria to identify the most viable option for detailed development.

03

Preliminary & Conceptual Design

Development of conceptual layouts, preliminary calculations, and cost estimates sufficient for project approval and stakeholder alignment.

04

Detailed Engineering Design

Production of complete design drawings, specifications, structural and hydraulic calculations, and all technical documents required for construction procurement.

05

Tender Support & Procurement Assistance

Preparation of tender packages, response to technical queries during bidding, and technical evaluation of contractor submissions.

06

Construction Support & Technical Advisory

Continued technical support during construction including design clarifications, site visits, review of shop drawings, and resolution of technical issues.
