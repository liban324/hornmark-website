Core Service

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning

Results-based monitoring and evaluation services that measure what matters — enabling evidence-based decision making and demonstrable impact for governments, NGOs, and international development partners.

Overview

Measuring Impact. Driving Improvement.

Horn Mark Design Consultant Ltd. provides comprehensive Monitoring, Evaluation and Learning (MEL) services for infrastructure projects, development programmes, and humanitarian interventions across Somalia and the Horn of Africa. Our MEL specialists design and implement evidence-based monitoring systems that generate reliable data to inform decision-making, demonstrate accountability, and drive continuous improvement.

We work with government agencies, UN organizations, NGOs, and bilateral donors to develop results frameworks, conduct baseline and endline studies, implement GIS-based monitoring, undertake third-party verification, and produce analytical reports that communicate programme performance and lessons learned.

HMD's MEL approach is grounded in international results-based management (RBM) frameworks and adapted for the challenging operating environment in Somalia, combining rigorous quantitative methods with qualitative community engagement to produce credible, actionable evidence.

Data & Technology

HMD uses GIS mapping, mobile data collection tools (KoBoToolbox, ODK), and data visualization platforms to deliver real-time monitoring dashboards and spatial analysis for programme management and donor reporting.

Key Deliverables

MEL Outputs

Baseline & Endline Reports

Rigorous baseline studies establishing reference points, and endline evaluations measuring change against indicators.

Monitoring Frameworks

Logframes, results frameworks, and indicator matrices with targets, sources of verification, and data collection plans.

GIS Monitoring Maps

Spatial analysis and mapping of project coverage, beneficiary locations, and infrastructure assets using GIS platforms.

Third-Party Monitoring Reports

Independent TPM reports verifying contractor deliverables, beneficiary numbers, and programme outcomes on behalf of donors.

Performance Dashboards

Real-time data visualization dashboards providing programme managers with live performance indicators and progress tracking.

Learning & After-Action Reports

Lessons learned documentation and knowledge management products supporting organizational learning and adaptive management.

Our Approach

The MEL Cycle

01

Design

Develop results framework, indicator definitions, data collection tools, and MEL plans aligned to programme objectives.

02

Collect

Field data collection through surveys, KIIs, FGDs, and remote sensing — using validated, ethical data collection methods.

03

Analyse

Quantitative and qualitative data analysis using statistical software and GIS to generate evidence-based findings.

04

Learn & Adapt

Share findings with stakeholders, support adaptive management decisions, and capture lessons for future programming.
