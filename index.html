Who We Are

A Trusted Partner in Engineering & Infrastructure

Horn Mark Design Consultant Ltd. (HMD) is a multidisciplinary engineering and consulting firm headquartered in Mogadishu, Somalia. Established in 2018, the firm provides integrated professional services in engineering consultancy, environmental and social assessment, construction management, infrastructure development, project supervision, and monitoring and evaluation.

With a team of experienced engineers, planners, environmental specialists, architects, and project management professionals, HMD delivers innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective solutions for public institutions, development partners, NGOs, private sector clients, and international organizations operating throughout Somalia and the Horn of Africa.

Our approach combines international best practices, advanced engineering technologies, local expertise, and a strong understanding of Somalia's operating environment to ensure successful project delivery from concept to completion.

Our Vision

To become the leading engineering, environmental, and construction management consulting firm in Somalia and the Horn of Africa, delivering sustainable infrastructure solutions that contribute to economic growth and community development.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality engineering, environmental, and project management services through innovation, technical excellence, integrity, and client-focused solutions that create lasting value for our stakeholders.

Digital Engineering

Building Information Modelling (BIM)

Horn Mark Design embraces Building Information Modelling (BIM) as a core component of modern engineering and construction project delivery. Through BIM-enabled workflows, we provide clients with improved project coordination, reduced construction risks, enhanced cost control, and optimized asset lifecycle management.

The adoption of BIM allows clients to minimize design conflicts, improve project coordination, and reduce construction risks — delivering better outcomes from design through to facility management.
