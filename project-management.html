Overview

Results-Driven Project Management

Horn Mark Design Consultant Ltd. provides comprehensive Project Management services that ensure projects are planned, executed, controlled, and closed with precision and professionalism. Our Project Managers bring internationally recognized expertise and apply structured methodologies to maintain full command of scope, schedule, cost, quality, and risk throughout every project phase.

We serve as owner's representatives, programme managers, and project support advisors across a wide range of infrastructure, development, and institutional projects in Somalia and the Horn of Africa. Our team coordinates between clients, contractors, consultants, and development partners to keep projects moving, aligned, and accountable.

Whether managing a single infrastructure project or overseeing a portfolio of development programmes, HMD brings structure, clarity, and results-oriented leadership that clients can rely on.

Methodologies & Tools

HMD applies internationally recognized PM frameworks including PMI PMBOK, PRINCE2, and results-based management (RBM) approaches commonly required by UN agencies, World Bank, EU, and bilateral donors.

Key Deliverables

Project Management Outputs

Project Management Plans

Comprehensive PMP covering scope, schedule, budget, quality, risk, communications, and procurement baselines.

Progress & Status Reports

Regular reporting packages keeping clients and stakeholders fully informed of project status, issues, and forecasts.

Risk Registers

Live risk registers with probability/impact ratings, mitigation strategies, owners, and status tracking throughout the project.

Cost Control Reports

Earned value analysis, cost-to-complete forecasts, variance analysis, and budget performance dashboards.

Programme Schedules

Detailed baseline and updated project schedules, critical path analysis, and look-ahead programmes.

Project Completion Reports

Final close-out documentation including lessons learned, performance summaries, and archival records.
